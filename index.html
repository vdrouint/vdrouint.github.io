My Research

Research

I maintain broad research interests. Part of my doctoral work has been on Monte-Carlo simulations of systems of classical spins, and part is on the exploration of electron pairing at Hund's coupled impurities.

You can find a list of my publications on my Google Scholar .

Hidden Order in Coupled XY Models

In this work, I performed large-scaled Monte-Carlo simulations and obtained the full phase diagram at finite coupling. We found a new phase of relative long-range Potts order where the individual variables were disordered. This can be understood through the confinement of fractional excitations.

Multiorbital Hund's coupled impurity.

I am developping a framework to understand how local spin fluctuations at the iron atoms can lead to superconductivity in the iron-based superconductors.
